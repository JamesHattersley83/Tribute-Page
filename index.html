Heres a Timeline of Eminem's Career

  • 1996 - Releases debut album Infinite but few people notice. Those who do accuse him of ripping off other artists. Those who don’t accuse him of plagiarism, and mock his little-too-late anti-Gulf War lyrics. He sells about five hundred copies from the backseat of his car.
  • Feb 1998 - Signs on with Dr. Dre’s Aftermath imprint.
  • Feb 1998 - Releases his second album The Slim Shady LP.
  • May 2000 - Releases third album The Marshall Mathers LP and in its first week it sells 1.76 million copies.
  • May 2002 - Releases fourth album The Eminem Show.
  • Nov 2004 - Releases his fifth album, Encore.
  • May 2009 - He releases his sixth album, Relapse. It debuts at #1 on the Billboard 200 chart.
  • June 2010 - He releases his seventh album, Recovery.
  • Nov 2013 - He releases his eighth album, The Marshall Mathers LP 2.
